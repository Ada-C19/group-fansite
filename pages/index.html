Community Based Practices

  • Ada's Mission

    • Ada’s mission is to prepare women and gender expansive adults to be software developers while advocating for inclusive and equitable work environments.
    • They primarily serve and address the needs of Black, Latine, Indigenous Americans, Native Hawaiian & Pacific Islander, LGBTQIA+, and low-income people.
    • Their tuition-free training program includes a collaborative learning environment, individualized support (mentors, tutors, a network of mental health supports, affinity groups), and an applied learning internship.

  • Our Connection to Community

    • Inspired by Ada's dedication to equity for the community, our fansite is dedicated to companies that center community needs.
    • We hope our site helps to highlight all the different community based organizations in and around Seattle.
