It's Her Heart That's Huge

The Far-reaching Philanthropy of Dolly Parton

Dolly Parton is widely known for her philanthropic efforts

and her commitment to giving back to the community.

She has established several charitable organizations and initiatives

that aim to make a positive impact on various causes.

Such highlights include:

  • 1986 - Dollywood Foundation

    Dolly Parton started the Dollywood Foundation with the goal of giving back to her hometown community in the Great Smoky Mountains.

    The foundation was initially established to support educational initiatives and improve the quality of life for the residents of Sevier County, Tennessee.

    One of the main driving forces behind the creation of the foundation was Dolly Parton's own humble upbringing and her belief in the transformative power of education.

    She wanted to provide opportunities and resources for children and families in the area, particularly in terms of early childhood literacy.

    This led to the development of the Imagination Library program, which has since expanded globally, providing free books to millions of children and fostering a love for reading.

    Dolly Parton's personal connection to her community and her passion for education and literacy have been the driving forces behind the Dollywood Foundation.

  • 1995 - Imagination Library

    The Imagination Library is one of the notable initiatives established by Dolly Parton.

    It is a book gifting program that aims to foster a love for reading among young children.

    Through the Imagination Library, registered children receive free age-appropriate books delivered to their homes on a monthly basis.

    The program started in 1995 and has since expanded globally, reaching millions of children worldwide.

    By providing access to books and promoting early literacy, the Imagination Library helps set children on a path of learning and imagination.

  • 2002 - Dolly Parton's Stampede Foundation

    The Dolly Parton's Stampede Foundation is an organization established by Dolly Parton to support various charitable causes.

    It aims to make a positive impact on local communities through initiatives focused on education, wellness, and community development.

    The foundation's efforts include providing scholarships, supporting local schools, and partnering with organizations that promote health and well-being.

    Through its philanthropic endeavors, the Dolly Parton's Stampede Foundation strives to improve the lives of individuals and communities in need.

    By leveraging Dolly Parton's platform and influence, the foundation brings attention to important social issues and supports impactful projects and programs.

  • 2016 - My People Fund

    Dolly Parton's My People Fund is a charitable initiative created to provide assistance to individuals and families affected by natural disasters and emergencies.

    The fund was established in 2016 following a devastating wildfire that affected the Great Smoky Mountains region in Tennessee.

    Its primary objective is to offer financial support to those who lost their homes or faced significant hardships as a result of the disaster.

    Through the My People Fund, Dolly Parton aims to provide immediate help and resources to rebuild and recover in times of crisis.

    The fund exemplifies Dolly Parton's commitment to her community and her dedication to helping those in need during challenging times.

  • 2018 - Dolly Parton's Pirates Voyage Foundation

    Dolly Parton's Pirates Voyage Foundation is a philanthropic organization established by the renowned entertainer to support various charitable causes and initiatives.

    The foundation focuses on making a positive impact on communities by providing assistance to those in need and promoting educational opportunities.

    Through partnerships and collaborations, the Pirates Voyage Foundation aims to improve the lives of individuals and families, particularly in the areas of education, healthcare, and disaster relief.

    With Dolly Parton's vision and passion for giving back, the foundation works to create meaningful and sustainable change in the communities it serves.

    The Pirates Voyage Foundation is another testament to Dolly Parton's unwavering commitment to philanthropy and her desire to make a difference in the world.
