Doodling

According to a extensive cognitive psychology studies, it has been proven that doodling can improve memory and concentration. "In fact, some researchers go as far to suggest that one of the benefits of doodling is that it triggers discoveries that words alone cannot produce. It can be an unexpected catalyst for discovery and growth."1

“You can use doodling as a tool ... to change your physical and neurological experience, in that moment."2
