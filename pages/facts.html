Background on the Show

SUGAR. SPICE. AND EVERYTHING NICE. The Powerpuff Girls aired from 1998 to 2005. It is an animated show following the adventures of three kindergarten-age sisters who have superpowers. They were created by Professor Utonium with a special ingredient called Chemical X, which allows them to fly and fight bad guys.
