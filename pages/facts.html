  • Best for big waves: Nazare, Portugal

    The largest wave ever surfed was in Nazare, Portugal. It was approximately 86 feet, and was surfed by Sebastian Steudtner.

  • Best for warm, chill waves: Noosa, Australia

    Noosa is famous for sheltered water and clean waves because of its small coves, protected by the Noosa Headlands. Noosa is experiencing an influx of potentially lethal jellyfish stings as species migrate due to climate change.

  • Best year-round surfing: Tofino, Canada

    Tofino covers over 20 miles of Pacific coastline with above-average waves.
