Early Life

Dolly Rebecca Parton was born on January 19, 1946 on Locust Ridge, Sevier County, TN. She grew up in a two-room cabin deep in Appalachia with 11 brothers and sisters. Her parents, Robert and Avie Lee Parton, had no idea their love of music would resonate so deeply with Dolly. Taking on the Knoxville airwaves while still in the single digits, those around her knew of her developing songwriting talent.


Career Start

Dolly's uncle, William Earl "Bill" Owens is the person responsible for setting Dolly on her trajectory. He pushed her to keep playing, keep writing, keep singing. You can read Dolly's touching tribute to her Uncle Bill to learn more about his influence.


Current Life

Dolly has been married to her husband, Carl Dean, for 55 years and they are still going strong! On the stage, Dolly's latest album, Rockstar, is her first ever rock album. It is currently available for pre-order here.
