Fun Facts You Should Know About Caffe Latte
One of the Most Popular Coffee Drinks in the World!

    home page latte art gallery

Caffe Latte, or simply known as "latte," originated in Italy in the 17th century. It is an espresso-based drink who's name translates to "milk coffee".

The espresso to milk ratio is: one-third espresso and two-thirds steamed milk. It has a small layer of milk foam on top.

The most popular Latte art design is called the "heart." It is a classic and widely recognized pattern that baristas create by pouring the steamed milk into the espresso in a specific manner.

If you order a Latte in Italy, you will recieve a glass of milk! Make sure you order a Caffe Latte specifically.
