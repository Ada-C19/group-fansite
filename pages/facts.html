Early Life

Dolly Rebecca Parton was born on January 19, 1946 on Locust Ridge, Sevier County, TN. She grew up in a two-room cabin deep in Appalachia with 11 brothers and sisters.
Her parents, Robert and Avie Lee Parton, had no idea their love of music would resonate so deeply with Dolly.
Taking on the Knoxville airwaves while still in the single digits, those around her knew of her
songwriting talent.
