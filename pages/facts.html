Here are some fun facts about Guy Fieri:

  • He was born on January 22, 1968.
  • His birth name is Guy Ramsay Ferry.
  • He is an American restaurateur, author, and television personality.
  • He rose to fame as the host of the TV show "Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives."
  • He is known for his signature spiky blonde hair and energetic personality.
