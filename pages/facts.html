Did you know these ten fun facts about bananas?

  1. A cluster of bananas is known as a hand.
  2. Technically, a banana is a type of berry!
  3. Bananas have a low density and can float in water.
  4. The most popular banana variety of the early 20th century was wiped out by a disease.
  5. Humans and bananas share about 50% of our DNA!
  6. Despite its appearance, the banana plant is not a tree.
  7. Because of their high potassium content, bananas emit a small amount of radiation.
  8. Bananas are native to Southeast Asia and Austronesia.
  9. Wild banana species can be pink, orange, or even striped.
  10. Bananas release a gas as they ripen that causes nearby fruits to ripen faster.
