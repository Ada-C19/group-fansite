Snoopy the Floofy

Facts about Snoopy

Snoopy is a 3 year old fluffy corgi that loves frolicking in big open spaces and stealing socks of his favorite people. Snoopy is always ready to give lots of kisses and bark at construction noises.

  • Favorite place is the beach!
  • Hates the rain!
  • Hates the heat!
  • Will eat anything!
