About

Navigating the Pork Plug Website

In our website you can find pork dishes from Chinese, Hawaiian, and Armenian culture. Each dish will have facts about the dish located in our Pork Facts page and pictures of each dish in our Pork Gallery page.
You may choose to contact us with pork dish suggestions, each suggestion needs to have at least three facts, 2 pictures, and a description about the dish. This description can be a personal story, impersonal story, or a recipe.

Your Neighborhood Pork Plugs
Pork Plugs was founded by 3 amazing women who are absolutely obssessed with all things pork. Dishes featured on this website are inspired by our own respective cultures. Our mission is to spread the good word of pork and dismantel the horrors of vegeterianism that continue to traumatize omnivours everywhere.
